# OMNIAMIND Boundaries

## Hard negatives

OMNIAMIND is not:

- consciousness
- artificial general intelligence
- a sentient system
- a semantic authority
- a truth engine
- a decision engine
- a replacement for expert judgment
- a replacement for OMNIA

---

## Boundary formula

```text
measurement != inference != decision
```

A measurement can constrain inference.

An inference can inform a decision.

A decision can use a signal.

But none of these layers are identical.

---

## Why the boundary matters

The word `mind` can easily be misread.

In this repository, `mind` does not mean consciousness.

It means an organized analytical interface around reasoning processes.

OMNIAMIND is therefore a structural cognition orchestration layer, not a claim of machine consciousness.

---

## Safe reading

The safe reading is:

```text
OMNIAMIND organizes.
OMNIA measures.
OMNIA-LIMIT interrupts.
Decision remains external.
```
