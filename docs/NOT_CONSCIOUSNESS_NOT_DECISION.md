# Not Consciousness, Not Decision

This document states the negative boundary explicitly.

OMNIAMIND is not artificial consciousness.

OMNIAMIND is not a decision engine.

OMNIAMIND is not a semantic truth oracle.

---

## Why this matters

The name OMNIAMIND can be misunderstood.

The repository should prevent that misunderstanding immediately.

Its role is orchestration:

    organize structural cognition workflows around measurement boundaries

It does not create an artificial subject.

It does not own final judgment.

It does not decide truth.

---

## What OMNIAMIND may do

OMNIAMIND may:

- organize observation workflows;
- route cases to OMNIA;
- route cases to OMNIA-RADAR;
- route cases to OMNIA-INVARIANCE;
- route cases to omnia-limit;
- route artifacts to OMNIA-VALIDATION;
- preserve explicit boundaries.

---

## What OMNIAMIND may not claim

OMNIAMIND should not claim:

- autonomous consciousness claim;
- subjective experience;
- autonomous judgment;
- semantic truth;
- final correctness;
- final decision authority.

---

## Boundary phrase

    workflow organization != consciousness
    workflow routing != truth
    measurement != inference != decision
    Decision remains external
